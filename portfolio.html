    Coding challenge : Week 3

    The conference ‘Beyond fair play: What would a fair society look like?’ looked at the various areas where action could be taken to make our society fairer. One of these was intergenerationnal inequalities and homeowner in recent years.

    It is quite remarkable that in the generations born in the 50s, the rate of people owning property exceeds 50% before the age of 30.

    However, we can see from the data that the threshold of 50% home ownership for the 1980s generation was not reached until well after the age of 30.

    As the conference concluded, inequalities are on the rise in the housing sector, and action on this issue is a relevant way of reducing intergenerational inequalities.
